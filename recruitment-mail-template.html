🎯 About K-1000

K-1000 is the Research and Development (R&D) initiative of KIIT, designed to foster innovation, research excellence, and real-world problem-solving. It aims to empower 1000 exceptional students through competitive training, collaborative research, project development, and professional growth across different schools.

Are you a strategic thinker with a passion for leadership?

We are excited to announce that we are officially opening applications for students to join the management teams of our organization's respective branches along with all the subdomains of the Event Management Branch and Central Teams for the upcoming academic year.

If you have a desire to make a significant impact on our campus community, we strongly encourage you to apply!
